ERP Design System

A complete redesign addressing readability, contrast, and professionalism. Light-dominant workspace with surgical green accents. Built for 8-hour workdays.

Design Rationale

What changed and why

Five critical problems in V1 that made the ERP feel harsh for daily use. Each fix improves readability and professional feel.

Problem 1 — Body text contrast
V1 body text on dark green
This text at #92AF95 on the dark gradient fails WCAG AA. Contrast ratio: 3.2:1
V2 fix: Dark green backgrounds are restricted to the sidebar only. All content text uses #3F424D on white — contrast ratio 9.8:1. Body text is never placed on dark green gradients.
Problem 2 — Amber on dark green
V1 EYEBROW
HEADING
V2 fix: Eyebrows and labels use #00B050 green on white backgrounds. The amber/solar gold #F0B429 is reserved for CTA buttons and specific warnings only — never as a label colour on dark surfaces.
Problem 3 — Green-tinted neutrals

V1 used olive-green neutrals everywhere: #92AF95, #668B6A, #456848. After 8 hours, everything looks grey-green and causes visual fatigue.

V2 fix: Content-area neutrals are desaturated warm-grays: #7C818E, #5A5E6B, #3F424D. Green is brand-only.
Problem 4 — Aggressive typography

V1 used OSWALD UPPERCASE for every heading — a condensed display face that feels industrial and aggressive in an everyday work tool.

V2 fix: DM Sans replaces Oswald for ERP headings. Geometric sans-serif: clean, modern, professional — no uppercase unless it's a label/eyebrow. Oswald is retained only for the marketing website hero.
Problem 5 — Dark green everywhere

V1 applied linear-gradient(150deg,#001F0D,#004D22,#007A38) to heroes, section backgrounds, sidebar, footer, table headers — making the entire app feel like one dark mass. Professional ERPs (Salesforce, Linear, Monday) are predominantly light.

V2 principle: The ERP workspace is 95% white/off-white. Dark surfaces exist only in the sidebar and data table headers. Brand green appears as lines, icons, badges, and buttons — never as a large background fill inside the app.
